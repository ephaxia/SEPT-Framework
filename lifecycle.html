Social Engineering Penetration testing lifecycle

Running a Social Engineering Penetration test requires following a set number of steps, these steps are proposed in the form of the following lifecycle.

Lifecycle of Penetration testing:

  1. Scope discussion with client

  2. Information gathering

  3. Establishing relationships & rapport with target

  4. Exploitatiosn

  5. Post exploitation Analysis

  6. Report discussion & suggestions for improvement

These lifecycles sections are not necessarily standalone and overlap may be seen with certain attacks. They are also not simply a one way lifecycle, regression may
occur from one section to another. For example, if not enough information is gathered for a particular exploit, you will need to regress to the information gathering phase
and re-engage in this activity until sufficient information is found. This increases the chances of the chosen exploit being successful.
